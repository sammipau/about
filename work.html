Decode the Melody

Decode the Melody is an exploratory data analysis visualization project centered on music traits and popularity, across various platforms and years. From the casual listener to the aspiring music producer, music shapes the cadences of daily life, defining moods and moments through rhythm, lyrics, and melody. Due to its immense global influence, there is a natural curiosity about musical patterns indicated by the growth of movements like Spotify Wrapped, or data-driven music applications like Receiptify. To address this growing curiosity, our visualization will allow users to explore the chemistry behind top songs across various platforms, helping anyone gain a comprehensive view of the current musical landscape.

Navigate

An all-in-one interactive map of the UBC campus where one can view the indoor accommodations (such as wheelchair accessibility), book a study room, and book an appointment with community primary care. The maps offer navigation routes, as well as visual representations of available study rooms.

Torrent Site

Our project goal is to create a torrent site to help users index and archive data through the bittorrent protocol. To achieve this, the service allows users to upload bittorrent files which point to the data itself along with metadata and tagging that allows other users to easily find the files. For example, a user could upload an Arch Linux ISO torrent, tag it with the “Arch” tag and place it within the “Linux ISO” category.
